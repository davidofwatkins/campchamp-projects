About Us

Welcome to CleanStart! We are a group of students studying at Champlain College. While our main compus is in Burlington, VT, we are studying Environmental Science this semester up at our campus in Montreal, Canada. After learning about the energy crisis in class, we felt that we could spread awareness of the benefits of renewable energies like solar and wind through a website. Thus, CleanStart was born. Thank you for visiting, and we hope our site makes a difference!

—David, Rebecca, & Brenna
