How much time do we have?

Our society has become extremely reliant upon fossil fuels and other forms of energy that do not only have a negative impact on the environment, but that we are also depleting far too quickly. Fortunately, there are several clean, renewable energies that are available to us. And as we consume more and more natural resources, we are going to have to depend upon renewable alternatives even more.
